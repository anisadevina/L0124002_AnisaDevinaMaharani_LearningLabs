Favorite Fashion Brands

Vivienne Westwood

A British fashion designer known for her punk-inspired designs and bold, unconventional approach to fashion. Her creations often feature historical influences, rebellious patterns, and avant-garde aesthetics.

Gucci

An iconic Italian luxury brand recognized for its high-quality craftsmanship and stylish designs. Known for its bold logos, distinctive patterns, and luxurious accessories, Gucci often blends contemporary trends with traditional craftsmanship.

Dior

A French luxury brand known for its elegant, high-fashion designs. Founded by Christian Dior in 1946, it’s famous for its iconic "New Look" and sophisticated collections in apparel, accessories, and fragrances.

Prada

An Italian fashion house famous for its sleek, minimalist style and cutting-edge designs. Prada is known for its high-fashion apparel, leather goods, and sophisticated yet innovative approach to design, often mixing modern aesthetics with classic luxury.

Givenchy

A French luxury brand known for its elegant, chic, and timeless fashion. Givenchy offers sophisticated collections ranging from haute couture to ready-to-wear, often with a refined, modern twist. The brand is associated with both high fashion and Hollywood glamour.

Axes Femme

A Japanese fashion brand known for its feminine, vintage-inspired style. Axes Femme combines soft, romantic elements with modern touches, creating pieces that are both cute and sophisticated, often featuring lace, bows, and pastel colors.

Liz Lisa

Another Japanese brand, Liz Lisa is famous for its sweet, girly aesthetic. The brand focuses on feminine, whimsical designs that often feature florals, lace, and bows. It has a cute, youthful vibe with a focus on creating flattering, delicate silhouettes.

Rosa Cheri

A brand that blends elegance and femininity with a focus on soft, romantic designs. Known for its use of lace, ruffles, and other delicate details, Rosa Chéri creates outfits that convey grace and charm, often incorporating pastel shades and floral motifs.
